FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to what most clients ask before starting.

How much does a website cost?

Most business websites land between GHS 4,000 and GHS 25,000 depending on scope; landing pages and simple sites start around GHS 800–4,000. Use our estimator to get an indicative range for your budget in about two minutes — final pricing is always confirmed in writing.

How long does a project take?

Landing pages typically take 1–2 weeks, most business websites 2–4 weeks, and larger builds 4–8 weeks. We confirm the timeline in your proposal before we start.

What does working with you look like?

A short strategy call, then a written scope and quote. We build in milestones with WhatsApp check-ins along the way, and finish with launch and a handover so you know how to use everything.

Is ad spend included in your fees?

No — ad spend goes directly to Meta and you stay in control of the budget (most clients start with GHS 500–2,000 per month). Our management fee covers strategy, targeting, creative, and optimization, and is quoted separately.

Do you offer maintenance after launch?

Yes. Optional monthly maintenance covers updates, backups, small content changes, and a monthly report — or we hand the site over fully and you run it yourself.

Do you work with businesses outside Ghana?

Yes — we serve growing businesses across West Africa, in English and French, with pricing shown in GHS or CFA (XOF).
